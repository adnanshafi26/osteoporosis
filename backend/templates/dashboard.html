About Osteoporosis

Osteoporosis is a condition where bones become weak and brittle due to reduced bone mineral density. This increases fracture risk, especially in the hip, spine, and wrist. Early detection using AI systems can help doctors identify high-risk patients and prevent fractures.

Healthy BMI Levels

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • Normal BMI: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30 and above

Bone Density Levels

  • Healthy: above 0.75
  • Moderate Risk: 0.60 – 0.75
  • High Risk: below 0.60

Recommended Nutrients

  • Calcium: 1000 – 1200 mg/day
  • Vitamin D: 600 – 800 IU/day
  • Magnesium and Zinc support bone metabolism
